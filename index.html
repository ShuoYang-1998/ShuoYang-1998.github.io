Biography

I am a first year bachelor-straight-to Ph.D. student in School of Electrical and Data Engineering, University Technology Sydney, advised by Prof. Min Xu. My research interests include developing revolutionary resource-efficient (data-efficient, annotation-efficient, and training-efficient) machine learning algorithms and applying them to downstream computer vision tasks.

I obtained my bachelor degree from School of Computer Science, Harbin Institute of Technology, Harbin, China in June 2020. I interned at JD AI Lab in 2019, ByteDance AI Lab in 2021, and Baidu Cognitive Computing Lab in 2021.
