Short Bio

I am currently a final year bachelor-straight-to-Ph.D. student at University of Technology Sydney. My research interests lie in data-centric AI, including but not limited to data efficiency (ICLR’21 Oral, TPAMI’21, ICLR'22, CVPR’22, ECCV’22), data reliability (ICML’22, arXiv’22b), data interpretability (arXiv’22a), data security, and so on. I have proposed several interesting research problems in data-centric AI, such as data distribution calibration (ICLR'21 Oral) and generalization-guaranteed dataset pruning (arXiv'22a). I was awarded the Google Ph.D. Fellowship in 2022.
