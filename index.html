Biography

I am a second year bachelor-straight-to-Ph.D. student (July 2020 - Jan. 2024) in University of Technology Sydney, Sydney, Australia, advised by Prof. Min Xu. I obtained my bachelor degree from School of Computer Science, Harbin Institute of Technology, Harbin, China in June 2020 (Aug. 2016 - June 2020). I interned at JD (JingDong) AI Lab in 2019, ByteDance AI Lab in 2021, and Baidu Cognitive Computing Lab in 2021.

I have broad research interests in developing revolutionary sample-efficient machine learning algorithms. Feel free to reach me if you have research interests similar to mine! I am open for collaboration and internship positions (also full-time positions after 2024)!
