Biography

I am a second year bachelor-straight-to-Ph.D. student (July 2020 - Jan. 2024) in School of Electrical and Data Engineering, University of Technology Sydney, Sydney, Australia, advised by Prof. Min Xu. I obtained my bachelor degree from School of Computer Science, Harbin Institute of Technology, Harbin, China in June 2020 (Aug. 2016 - June 2020). I interned at JD (JingDong) AI Lab in 2019, ByteDance AI Lab in 2021, and Baidu Cognitive Computing Lab in 2021.

My research interests include developing revolutionary resource-efficient (data-efficient, annotation-efficient, and training-efficient) machine learning algorithms, e.g., few-shot learning, meta-learning, label-noise learning, incremental learning, partial-label learning, complementary-label learning, dataset condensation (learning to synthesis a much smaller dataset from raw real dataset with hopefully no sacrifice in performance) etc., and applying them to downstream computer vision tasks. Welcome to contact me if you have research interests similar to mine! I am open for collaboration and internship positions (also full-time positions after 2024)!
